How IntelliSurge Works

Our system is built on a robust, multi-layered technology stack designed for accuracy, scalability, and real-time performance.

1

Data Ingestion & Integration

We aggregate data from multiple sources: historical hospital admission records (EHRs), real-time public health alerts (e.g., CDC, local health departments), and environmental data like weather patterns and seasonal flu activity.

2

AI-Powered Predictive Modeling

Our core machine learning model (utilizing time-series analysis like LSTM) processes this data to identify patterns and accurately forecast patient admission volumes and types of care required up to 72 hours in advance.

3

Actionable Insights & Visualization

The predictions are translated into simple, actionable recommendations for staffing, resource allocation, and operational protocols, presented through our intuitive web and mobile dashboards for hospital administrators and staff.
